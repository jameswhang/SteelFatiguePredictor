The Steel Fatigue Predictor Project started out as a Design Thinking and Communication class project at Northwestern University in Spring 2014. The class project was carried out as a proof of concept that such thing can be done. After a year, the project was carried out even further by applying better methods and making a user interface like this website for peope to actually use our model. This project is currently under the hands of two Northwestern students: James Whang (CS '17) and Marisa You (MSE '17).
