The Steel Fatigue Predictor


The Center for Hierarchical Materials Design consists of a hierarchical tool sets that can be used to bring down the development cycle of new materials. We focus on taking the processed data and proto data in the Materials Genome section of the hierarchical toolsets and generating a predictive modelfor mechanical property of the steel: fatigue strength in particular.

Predicting fatigue strength is important in particular because testing for fatigue strength is heavy in both time and resource consumption.
